GradRags

Grad Rags is a company by students, for students. Our goal is simple: to eliminate the unnecessary environmental and financial costs associated with graduation caps and gowns. We understand that not everyone has the financial resources to pay a substantial price for a brand new cap and gown that they will only wear once in their life. Therefore, we are here to offer a high quality, like-new product significantly discounted to help save both your pocketbook and our environment. Most importantly, we organize a charitable clothing drive and donate portions of our profits to local homeless shelters because we understand that we are blessed just to be able to have the clean clothes on our backs.


FocUs

The system, written in Visual Basic .NET, allows the user to create a profile that specifies what his/her primary task will be and what applications, programs, or websites should be considered on-task and off-task. The system then uses this input to drive an application monitoring module that tracks the user’s on-task and off-task behaviors. The profile system also allows the user to choose a maximum break time for each task. Once the user exceeds this threshold, a trigger is presented to provide the user with the choice of continuing his/her break or resuming work on the primary task.
